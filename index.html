Get the program design anyway. You'll have a concrete document to plan against — useful for the next budget cycle, headcount planning, or for comparing vendors. There's no commitment on either side.

How is this different from what we already buy?

Most vendors sell workshops. We design capability systems. After understanding your brief, we match you with the right expert, design a custom programme, and build in reinforcement so training actually changes behaviour.
